Artist’s Statement

 

每個人年少時總不免浪費時光。影片中的主角在夢中穿越到十年後，發現自己因以往虛耗光陰而將來需要過著難受的生活後，醒後決心改過。

Everyone may have wasted time when they were young. In this video, the boy is dreaming his miserable life in the future due to his waste of time in the past. When he wakes up, he decides to change.
